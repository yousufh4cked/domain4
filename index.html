About

A mind built for healing, a heart shaped by knowledge.

I’m Samit Zayed — a medical student at Chittagong Medical College, pursuing my MBBS with unwavering dedication. Based in Chittagong, Bangladesh, I’ve always believed that medicine is more than a profession; it’s a calling.

My academic journey is uniquely diverse — alongside medicine, I’ve studied Applied Mathematics at NSTU and pursued formal Islamic scholarship through IOM and Taibah Academy. This interdisciplinary background gives me analytical rigor, ethical grounding, and compassionate care.

I live by my motto — sacrificing nights to save lives — and I channel every sleepless hour into becoming the best physician I can be.
